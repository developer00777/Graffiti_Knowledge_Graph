# HR Process Automation - Discovery Questionnaire

1. What are the top 5 most repetitive and time-consuming tasks your team performs daily/weekly?
2. What tools and systems does your team currently use? (e.g., Excel, SAP, Workday, email, HRIS portal)
3. When a new employee joins, what documents do you collect and how do you receive them? (email, portal, physical)
4. How is the new employee's data entered into your systems — and how long does it take per employee?
5. Are offer letters, appointment letters, and experience letters generated manually or from templates?
6. How are leave requests and attendance currently tracked, approved, and reconciled?
7. What inputs do you manually collect and consolidate each month for payroll processing?
8. What does your employee exit/offboarding process look like — is there a manual checklist you follow?
9. What periodic reports do you generate (headcount, attrition, compliance), and how are they compiled?
10. How are employee data change requests (bank details, address, nominee) received and updated?
11. Is any data duplicated across multiple systems that your team has to keep in sync manually?
12. Are most of your tasks rule-based (clear steps, no judgment needed) or do they require human decision-making?
13. What is the current error rate in manual data entry, and what happens when mistakes are found?
14. Which single task, if automated tomorrow, would save your team the most time?
15. Are there documented SOPs or templates for your key processes that we can review?
